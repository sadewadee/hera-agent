# Game Narrative

## Purpose
Design game narratives, write dialogue, and create branching storylines.

## Instructions
1. Define the story premise and themes
2. Create characters with motivations and arcs
3. Design the narrative structure (linear, branching, open)
4. Write dialogue and cutscene scripts
5. Integrate narrative with gameplay mechanics

## Narrative Structures
- **Linear**: Fixed story progression
- **Branching**: Player choices affect story path
- **Open world**: Emergent narrative from gameplay
- **Hub and spoke**: Central narrative with optional side stories
- **Environmental**: Story told through world details

## Best Practices
- Show, don't tell (use environmental storytelling)
- Player agency should feel meaningful
- Keep dialogue natural and character-appropriate
- Test narrative pacing with playtesters
- Balance story and gameplay seamlessly
