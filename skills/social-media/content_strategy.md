# Content Strategy

## Purpose
Plan and execute social media content strategies across platforms with consistent messaging and measurable goals.

## Instructions
1. Define brand voice and content pillars
2. Research audience and platform demographics
3. Create a content calendar with themes and formats
4. Plan content production workflow
5. Define KPIs and measurement framework

## Content Pillars
- Educational: teach your audience something valuable
- Entertaining: engage with humor, stories, or trends
- Inspirational: motivate with success stories or vision
- Promotional: showcase products or services (max 20%)
- Community: engage with audience content and conversations

## Best Practices
- Maintain consistent posting frequency
- Repurpose content across platforms with adaptation
- Engage with audience comments and messages
- Track metrics weekly and adjust strategy monthly
- Stay authentic to brand voice
