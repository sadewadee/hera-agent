# Hashtag Strategy

## Purpose
Research and optimize hashtag usage for social media visibility and engagement.

## Instructions
1. Research relevant hashtags for your niche
2. Analyze hashtag popularity and competition
3. Create a hashtag strategy by platform
4. Mix broad and niche hashtags
5. Track performance and adjust

## Strategy
- Use 3-5 broad hashtags for reach
- Use 5-10 niche hashtags for targeted audience
- Create branded hashtags for campaigns
- Rotate hashtags to avoid being flagged as spam
- Research competitor hashtag usage

## Best Practices
- Research before using any hashtag
- Avoid banned or controversial hashtags
- Vary hashtags across posts
- Track which hashtags drive engagement
- Update hashtag lists monthly
