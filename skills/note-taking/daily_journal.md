# Daily journaling and reflection

## Purpose
Daily journaling and reflection for effective knowledge capture and personal reflection.

## Instructions
1. Set up the note-taking structure
2. Capture information consistently
3. Review and process notes regularly
4. Link related concepts
5. Archive and retrieve efficiently

## Method
- Consistent daily or session-based capture
- Structured format for easy scanning
- Regular review for reinforcement
- Linking to related notes and sources
- Periodic archiving and pruning

## Best Practices
- Write in your own words for better retention
- Keep notes concise and actionable
- Review regularly for reinforcement
- Use templates for consistency
- Back up notes to multiple locations
