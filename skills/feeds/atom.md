# Atom feed management

## Purpose
Atom feed management for staying informed and managing content consumption.

## Instructions
1. Identify feed sources and subscription criteria
2. Configure feed parsing and content extraction
3. Set up filtering and categorization rules
4. Manage notifications and digest scheduling
5. Archive and organize consumed content

## Feed Management
- Subscribe to feeds by URL or topic discovery
- Categorize feeds into folders or tags
- Set update frequency per feed
- Filter content by keywords, authors, or topics
- Export subscriptions as OPML for portability

## Content Processing
- Extract full article content from feed entries
- Summarize long articles for quick scanning
- Detect duplicate content across feeds
- Identify trending topics across subscriptions
- Archive important articles for later reference

## Notification Strategy
- Real-time alerts for high-priority feeds
- Daily or weekly digest emails for regular feeds
- Keyword-triggered notifications for specific topics
- Quiet hours and notification batching
- Cross-platform notification delivery

## Best Practices
- Regularly prune inactive or low-value feeds
- Use feed aggregation to reduce noise
- Tag articles for easy retrieval later
- Set reading time budgets to avoid information overload
- Export highlights and notes for reference
