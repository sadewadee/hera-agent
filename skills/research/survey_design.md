# Survey design and analysis

## Purpose
Survey design and analysis for high-quality academic and professional research.

## Instructions
1. Define the research objective
2. Select appropriate tools and methods
3. Design with rigor and validity in mind
4. Execute and collect data systematically
5. Analyze and report findings

## Best Practices
- Use validated instruments and methods
- Document methodology for reproducibility
- Follow ethical guidelines
- Report all results transparently
- Peer review before publishing
