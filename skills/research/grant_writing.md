# Grant and proposal writing

## Purpose
Grant and proposal writing for high-quality academic and professional research.

## Instructions
1. Define the research question and scope
2. Design the appropriate methodology
3. Collect and analyze data rigorously
4. Synthesize findings with proper citations
5. Present results clearly and accurately

## Research Process
- Literature review and gap identification
- Hypothesis formulation
- Study design and methodology selection
- Data collection and management
- Analysis and interpretation
- Writing and peer review
- Publication and dissemination

## Quality Standards
- Reproducible methods and analysis
- Proper statistical procedures
- Transparent reporting of all results
- Adequate sample sizes with power analysis
- Pre-registration when appropriate

## Citation Standards
- Use consistent citation style (APA, MLA, Chicago, IEEE)
- Cite primary sources when available
- Include DOIs for digital references
- Manage references with tools (Zotero, Mendeley)
- Verify all citations are accurate

## Best Practices
- Keep detailed research notes and lab notebooks
- Use version control for manuscripts and code
- Share data and code for reproducibility
- Follow ethical guidelines for human/animal research
- Engage with peer feedback constructively
