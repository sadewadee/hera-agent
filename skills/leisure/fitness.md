# Fitness routines and exercise planning

## Purpose
Fitness routines and exercise planning for personal enrichment and leisure activities.

## Instructions
1. Understand the user's preferences and constraints
2. Provide personalized recommendations based on criteria
3. Include practical details and actionable steps
4. Offer alternatives for different budgets and time frames
5. Suggest related activities and resources

## Recommendations
- Personalized based on stated preferences
- Filtered by available time, budget, and constraints
- Ranked by relevance and quality
- Include variety for exploration
- Reference trusted review sources

## Planning
- Step-by-step guidance for activities
- Required materials, tools, or preparations
- Time estimates for completion
- Difficulty level and skill requirements
- Safety considerations and precautions

## Best Practices
- Ask clarifying questions to narrow recommendations
- Provide options at different difficulty levels
- Include seasonal and location-based suggestions
- Update recommendations based on feedback
- Respect dietary, physical, and personal preferences
