# Email deliverability optimization

## Purpose
Email deliverability optimization for effective email communication and management.

## Instructions
1. Analyze the email communication requirements
2. Apply best practices for the specific email task
3. Implement with proper formatting and structure
4. Test deliverability and rendering across clients
5. Track and optimize based on engagement metrics

## Email Best Practices
- Clear, actionable subject lines (under 50 characters)
- Single call-to-action per email
- Mobile-responsive design
- Plain text alternative for all HTML emails
- Unsubscribe link for marketing emails (CAN-SPAM compliance)

## Technical Standards
- SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication
- Proper List-Unsubscribe headers
- HTML email compatibility across clients
- Image optimization and alt text
- Preheader text optimization

## Deliverability
- Maintain clean sender reputation
- Warm up new sending domains gradually
- Monitor bounce rates and remove invalid addresses
- Avoid spam trigger words and excessive punctuation
- Use a consistent from address and sending schedule

## Testing
- Test rendering in major email clients (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail)
- Check spam score before sending
- A/B test subject lines and content
- Verify all links and tracking codes
- Test on mobile and desktop

## Best Practices
- Segment audiences for targeted messaging
- Respect recipient preferences and frequency caps
- Track key metrics: open rate, click rate, unsubscribe rate
- Implement proper consent management
- Archive sent emails for compliance
