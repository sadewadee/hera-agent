# Exercise Planning

## Purpose
Plan exercise routines and track workouts for fitness goals.

## Important Disclaimer
This is for informational purposes only. Consult a healthcare provider before starting any exercise program.

## Instructions
1. Assess current fitness level and goals
2. Design an appropriate exercise program
3. Track workouts and progress
4. Adjust based on results and recovery
5. Maintain consistency with sustainable habits

## Program Design
- Progressive overload for strength gains
- Periodization for long-term progress
- Balance strength, cardio, and flexibility
- Include rest and recovery days
- Adapt to individual needs and constraints

## Best Practices
- Start conservative and progress gradually
- Prioritize form over weight or speed
- Track workouts for accountability
- Listen to your body for recovery signals
- Vary routines to prevent plateaus
