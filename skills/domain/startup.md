# Startup Guidance

## Purpose
Provide guidance for startup founders on business planning, product development, fundraising, and growth strategies.

## Instructions
1. Understand the startup stage and challenges
2. Provide relevant frameworks and guidance
3. Suggest actionable next steps
4. Connect to relevant resources and examples
5. Identify common pitfalls to avoid

## Startup Stages
- **Ideation**: Problem validation, market research, team formation
- **MVP**: Minimum viable product, early users, feedback loops
- **Product-Market Fit**: Retention metrics, growth signals, iteration
- **Growth**: Scaling team, processes, and infrastructure
- **Scale**: Optimization, expansion, fundraising rounds

## Key Frameworks
- Lean Startup: Build-Measure-Learn cycle
- Jobs To Be Done: understand customer motivations
- Business Model Canvas: visualize business model
- OKRs: set and track objectives and key results
- Customer Development: validate before building

## Best Practices
- Talk to customers before building
- Focus on one problem and solve it well
- Measure what matters (not vanity metrics)
- Move fast but make reversible decisions
- Build a team with complementary skills
