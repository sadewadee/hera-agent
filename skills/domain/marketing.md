# Digital Marketing

## Purpose
Plan and execute digital marketing strategies including SEO, content marketing, paid advertising, and growth tactics.

## Instructions
1. Define marketing objectives and target audience
2. Select channels based on audience behavior
3. Create campaign strategy with measurable KPIs
4. Execute and monitor campaigns
5. Analyze results and optimize

## Channels
- **SEO**: Organic search visibility and traffic
- **Content Marketing**: Blog, video, podcast, social content
- **Paid Search (SEM)**: Google Ads, Bing Ads
- **Social Advertising**: Meta, LinkedIn, Twitter ads
- **Email Marketing**: Newsletters, drip campaigns
- **Influencer Marketing**: Partnerships and sponsorships

## Analytics
- Track funnel metrics (awareness, consideration, conversion)
- Attribution modeling for multi-touch journeys
- A/B testing for creative and messaging
- ROI calculation per channel
- Cohort analysis for retention

## Best Practices
- Define clear KPIs before launching campaigns
- Test small, scale what works
- Focus on customer lifetime value, not just acquisition
- Build a first-party data strategy
- Stay compliant with privacy regulations
