# Meeting management and facilitation

## Purpose
Meeting management and facilitation for improved personal and professional effectiveness.

## Instructions
1. Assess current practices and pain points
2. Apply the appropriate methodology
3. Implement changes incrementally
4. Track progress and adjust
5. Build sustainable habits

## Methodology
- Clear objectives and success criteria
- Structured approach with flexibility
- Regular review and adjustment
- Accountability and tracking
- Sustainable practices over quick fixes

## Best Practices
- Start with one technique and master it
- Track progress to maintain motivation
- Adapt methods to your personal style
- Protect time for focused work
- Review and iterate weekly
