# Typography design and font selection

## Purpose
Provide expert guidance and tools for typography projects.

## Instructions
1. Understand the creative requirements and constraints
2. Apply relevant design principles and techniques
3. Generate or review work with structured feedback
4. Iterate based on critiques
5. Deliver in the appropriate format

## Design Principles
- Balance aesthetics with functionality
- Follow established conventions for the medium
- Consider the target audience and context
- Iterate based on feedback
- Document design decisions

## Best Practices
- Research before creating
- Use reference material for inspiration
- Test on the target medium/device
- Get feedback from diverse perspectives
- Maintain consistency across the project
